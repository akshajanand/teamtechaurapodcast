From fake delivery messages to "investment schemes" that promise 5,000 rupees for investing 500, the team discusses the tactics scammers use to bypass your logic and attack your emotions: fear, excitement, and urgency.

Top Red Flags Discussed

  • Spelling Mistakes: Professional companies rarely make grammar errors.
  • Urgency: "Click NOW or your account is blocked!" is a classic fear tactic.
  • Unknown Links: Always check the URL. If it doesn't match the official site, don't click.
  • Requests for Personal Info: No reputable bank asks for your full details or OTP via SMS.
